Hi, I'm Swarnika Srivastava

A self-made digitalpreneur, creative strategist, full-time MNC professional, and the founder of Buylogie Digital Marketing Solutions.

My Journey

My journey didn't begin in a fancy office - it began late at night after MNC work hours, with just my laptop, a cup of chai, and a vision. Like many 9-to-5 women, I craved more: more freedom, more fulfillment, more impact. That dream led me into freelancing.

What started as a weekend hustle quickly evolved into purpose-driven digital work. I began helping local real estate ventures and gym businesses elevate their online game - designing scroll-stopping visuals, crafting lead-gen strategies, and building brands that actually converted.

Soon, I founded Buylogie - a boutique digital marketing brand that now helps solopreneurs, modern creators, and small businesses thrive online. At Buylogie, we don't just design - we engineer digital growth.
